Shop Log File Parser

This tool lets you convert your shop log files from Stoneworks into easily consumable formats with bots (JSON) or spreadsheets (CSV).
After clicking on shop chest/barrels in-game to see their available stock and price, you can upload your log file here to parse the shop inventories and prices, and convert it into JSON or CSV format.
You can either drag and drop the file into the dropzone below or click to upload.

Supports either .log files, or .gz archives!
